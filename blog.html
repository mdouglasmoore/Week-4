• #6 ON TRENDING FOR COLLEGES

    VanArts: an educational experience in online academics

    Learning the bare bones of web design & development.

    Article written by Matthew Douglas

    Saturday | 11/04/2023



    Creativity and practicality. Two things that tend to contradict each other in this modern-day era. Careers in innovation and imagination often accompany a life of risk taking and the constant uncertainty of financial instability, whereas the latter can quickly slip to the mundane, repetitive and unrewarding. However, the Vancouver Institute of Media Arts manages to harmonize these counteracting concepts with ease through one single program. Web design & Development.

    From the first moment of stepping foot on campus, the enthusiasm is undeniable. Everyone around you seems to be engulfed in the excitement of unknown possibilities. Being greeted personally by friendly, high-ranking members of the faculty and getting introduced to every peer of yours within the first hour only enhances the feeling that you are part of something special. A place that takes pride in making sure you feel comfortable, confident, and prepared for this next chapter you are about to embark on. Somewhere that you belong.

    Blessed with the teachings of a knowledgeable and experienced staff, the art of designing and developing websites quickly transforms from overwhelming to almost effortless. Programs like Photoshop and VSC are integrated into this course perfectly with ease in ways that even an absolute novice such as myself can comprehend.

    With only two months down and ten more to go, I am eagerly looking forward to what the future holds at VanArts.
